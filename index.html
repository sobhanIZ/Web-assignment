About KidsWhoCode

Hi and welcome to KidsWhoCode which is a national charity that aims at preparing children and young people for the world of technology. Our goal is to deliver these coding and programming resources to schools and educators interested in training children from 8-14 years of age for free of charge. It is for this reason that we envision every child, irrespective of his or her background, being able to acquire fundamental computing skills that will enable him or her work effectively in the arising complexities of technology related careers and pursue STEM careers of his or her choice.

As tutors at KidsWhoCode, we work with talented team of enthusiastic teachers and practitioners from STEM field to develop relevant, fun and inclusive lessons. We are available for a teacher willing to create captivating lessons, for a student keen to master coding, or a volunteer ready to perform a change.
